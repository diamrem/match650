Hong Cheng is an Assistant Professor in Department of Systems Engineering and Engineering Management at the Chinese University of Hong Kong. She received her Ph.D. in Computer Science from University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign in 2008. Before that, she received her M.Phil. degree from Hong Kong University of Science and Technology in 2003, and B.S. degree from Zhejiang University in 2001.
